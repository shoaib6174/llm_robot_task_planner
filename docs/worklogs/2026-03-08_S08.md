## Work Done

### PID Tuning — Joints 3/4/5
- Increased cmd_max from 5 to 20 N·m for joints 3/4 and to 20 for joint5
- Increased P gain from 50 to 100 for joints 3/4/5
- Added I gain (I=50 for joints 1/2, I=30 for joints 3/4/5) with i_max clamping to eliminate P-only steady-state error
- Joint4 was stuck at 0 when joints 2/3 moved simultaneously — coupling torques from upstream motion overwhelmed cmd_max=5

### Staged Joint Commands
- Refactored `send_pose()` to send upstream joints (1-2) first, wait 1.5s, then downstream joints (3-5)
- This prevents coupling torques from overwhelming lightweight downstream controllers
- Increased SETTLE_TIME from 2.0 to 3.0s

### Gripper Fix
- r_joint had damping=10.0 with inertia ~4e-7 kg·m² — same damping/inertia ratio issue as arm joints but 50x worse
- Lowered r_joint damping to 0.001, friction to 0.001
- Added dynamics (damping=0.001, friction=0.001) to all 5 mimic joints
- Added I gain (P=100, I=30) to gripper PID controller
- Gripper now opens (0.5 rad) and closes (-0.2 rad) reliably

### Joint5 Coupling
- Gripper motion creates coupling torque on joint5 (wrist rotation, same Z axis)
- Increased joint5 cmd_max from 10 to 20 to partially resist coupling
- Residual drift of ~0.25 rad remains but is cosmetic for demo

### Full Pick/Place Test
- Pick sequence: gripper open → pre_grasp → grasp → gripper close → lift — all steps complete
- Place sequence: place position → gripper open → home — all steps complete
- Joint tracking within ~5-10% for most joints (PI control)

## Decisions Made
| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| PI control instead of P-only | P-only left ~10% steady-state error from gravity/friction; I term eliminates it |
| Stage joint commands (upstream first) | Simultaneous motion caused coupling torques that overwhelmed downstream controllers |
| Very low gripper damping (0.001) | Gripper link inertia ~4e-7 kg·m²; even damping=0.5 creates ratio >1M causing DART numerical issues |
| Leave link4/servo_link2/camera collision meshes removed | Not needed for demo; may cause self-collision limiting joint2 range |
| Accept joint5 coupling drift from gripper | Cosmetic issue only; would need more complex decoupling to fix |
